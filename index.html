The Concept — IUAIYUUU

IUAIYUUU isn’t just a name — it’s a reflection of balance.
“IU” represents warmth, empathy, and emotion — inspired by the artistry and sincerity of Lee Ji-eun.
“AI” represents logic, precision, and curiosity — the world of artificial intelligence that drives my academic and creative journey.
And “YUUU” is me — the human in between, blending emotion and computation, art and science.

This portfolio is built upon the idea that technology can be gentle — that systems can understand, and that design can listen. Like IU’s music, I want my work to feel calm, genuine, and deeply human.

“AI that listens, design that feels.”
